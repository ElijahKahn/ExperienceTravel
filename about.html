About Us

Experience traveling how your friends and family do.

At Experience Travel, we believe that traveling is best when you know the experience of how others travel. And hows the best way of doing that? Who has more similar taste in adventures and activities than your friends and family. With experience able to follow what type of activities and trips your friends and family take.

Our goal is to help people know exactly where they want to travel. Not only is finding destination extremely hard, finding activities and attractions at that destination is also hard. That's why we're here to help. Giving users access to see what destinations, activities and attractions their friends and families really enjoyed, allowed the to narrow down their search when planning trips.

This allows you to experience great trips and have the best time without having to think about if you made the right decision on where you're headed or what activities you chose to do.
